Welcome to my world journey.

My name is Alaa Shahin, I'm 23 years old and I born and raised in a small city in Syria called Homs. I lived there until I graduated High School. Then, my father decided to leave Syria to protect us from the war. We went to Kuala Lumpur the capital city of Malaysia in 2015. It was the most beautiful city I have ever been to. We spend a year and a half then we had to move to the United States. In June 2017, we entered the United States, and here when the journey began. I went to Oakland Community College for two years to receive my two associate degrees then I transferred to the University of Michigan to complete my education at the School of Information.

Kuala Lumpur colloquially referred to as KL, is a federal territory and the capital city of Malaysia. It is the largest city in Malaysia, covering an area of 243 km2 (94 sq mi) with an estimated population of 1.73 million as of 2016.[8] Greater Kuala Lumpur, also known as the Klang Valley, is an urban agglomeration of 7.564 million people as of 2018. It is among the fastest-growing metropolitan regions in Southeast Asia, in both population and economic development.
